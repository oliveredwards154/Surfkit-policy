Privacy Policy Last updated: 11/06/2025 Thank you for using SurfKit. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect the personal and device information you provide when you use our mobile application available on the Google Play Store ("App"). By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy. 1. Information We Collect We collect only the information you choose to provide to us. This includes: 1.1 Account Information Email address, name, and password you submit when you sign up for a BoardApp account. Google sign‑in name and email (if you choose to sign up or log in with Google). 1.2 Location Data Device location (latitude and longitude) to display nearby surf shops and to provide directions. 1.3 Photos and Camera Access Optional access to your camera and photo library if you choose to create or manage a surf shop listing and upload images of your shop. 1.4 Shop Owner Contact Details Optional contact information for surf shop listings you create, including phone number, email address, Facebook profile, Instagram handle, and physical shop address. No other data is collected by the App. All information collected is provided directly by you, and any data displayed in the App (e.g., shop listings) comes from you or other users who choose to share that information. 2. How We Use Your Information We use the information you provide to: Create and authenticate your account. Show your location relative to nearby surf shops. Enable shop owners to upload and display images of their shops. Allow shop owners to share their contact details and address for customer inquiries. Improve and personalize the App experience. We do not use your information for advertising purposes, analytics beyond aggregated performance metrics, or sell your personal data to any third party. 3. Data Sharing and Disclosure We do not share your personal information with third parties, except: Service providers who help us operate the App (e.g., cloud hosting, email notifications). Security We implement commercially reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no internet transmission is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security. 6. Children’s Privacy Our App is not intended for children under 13. Changes to This Privacy Policy We may update this policy from time to time.
